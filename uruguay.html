The Country of Uruguay

Uruguay has some unique features that contribute to its successes and failures in the distribution of laptops to every child.

Ninis

Deriving from the spanish "ni trabajar, ni estudiar" (neither work, nor study), ninis in Uruguay are a major problem for society. Only one in three students that start high school graduate and as nini suggests, many of them do not join the workforce.

Compulsory Education

Education is required for 10 years in Uruguay, which is average relative to other countries in South America.
